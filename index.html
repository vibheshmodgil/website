Recent Projects

Project 1

AI ML Based Motor Design Platfrom

Drive Cycle Analysis to obtain design inputs for the Motor and Battery – Generalized formulation of Lumped Parameter models for Thermal Analysis of Motors – Graphic User Interface of Motor Design software using Tkinter and standard Python Libraries

Project 2

Lumped Parameter Modelling of Motors for TMEIC

Generate Thermal Circuit for electrical motors to get fast estimation of temperatures – Solving Standard Heat Transfer Differential Equations Analytically to find the Resistances – Generate Codes to solve thermal circuit in MATLAB and Python – Validation of results through Ansys WorkBench

Project 3

Two Wheeler EV Drive Train Sizing

This report provides a comprehensive procedure for estimating battery size, design and optimization of motor specifications, fuel consumption and emission analysis from the raw drive cycle data. Raw drive cycle data is collected from different parts of India and comprises of both urban and rural drive data, this report is anticipated to represent the Indian driving scenario.

Project 4

Accessibility of Electric Vehicle in Indian Marke

The "Acceptability of Electric Vehicles in Indian Market" reports the public’s view and expectations towards electric mobility in the current scenario when there is a rapid shift towards greener mobility. – This survey aims to understand the actual demands of the consumer so that we can bridge the gap between consumer expectations and the 2 Introduction products developed by OEMs
